Best Movies in Nigeria 2023

These are my top 3 movies

No. 1 Anikulapo

From the bowels of legendary filmmaker Kunle Afolayan, ANIKULAPO is birthed and it will take you on an emotional trip that you are not ready for. The perfect mix of suspense, thrill, romance and tragedy.

No. 2 Brotherhood

After years of fighting to survive on the streets of Lagos, two brothers fall on opposite sides of the law. The bonds of brotherhood are put to the ultimate test as one joins a Taskforce that hunts down the other and his gang.

No. 3 Gangs of Lagos

A group of friends who each have to navigate their own destiny, growing up on the bustling streets and neighborhood of Isale Eko, Lagos.
